Harrys Hamsters


The Harry Hill Movie

Adventure | Comedy | Family

Description

When he thinks his hamster Ab (Johnny Vegas) has just a week to live, Harry Hill (Harry Hill) and his Nan (Dame Julie Walters) take the hamster to Blackpool. However, Harry's twin Otto (Matt Lucas) wants to kidnap Abu, and sends Ed (Simon Bird) and Kisko (Guillaume Delauney) to pursue them.

Director: Steve Bendelack

Writers: Jon Foster, Harry Hill, James Lamont.

Stars: Harry Hill, Julie Walters, Johnny Vegas.
